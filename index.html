      "Online Study Habits"
  • Finish Assigned Task Immediately
  • - So that the things to do won't pile up and you will have extra time to do other things.
  • More Reaserch
  • - Since it is online you can access the internet or web to suppplement the topics discussed by the professor.
  • Make a to do List
  • - Making a to do list will help you record the things needed to do so that you will know what to prioritize.
  • Take Note
  • - To help you to recap the discussion.
  • Make Studying Enjoyable
  • - So that you will be more proactive in the discussion.
  • Rest and Relax
  • - It is important so that we will be able to properly focus and keep ourselves healthy.
  • Ask for Clarifications
  • - Since it is an online class there will be problems that will hinder you on properly understanding the discussion, with asking you can clarify if you heard the things said by the professor correctly.
  • Study Group
  • - Having a study group will greatly help because you can ask your friends or classmate to teach you, since there will be certain lessons or topics that are very hard to understand and professors that is hard to approach or ask.
